  G       D    G D        Em    D       Bm

    G            D      G     D         Em     D     Bm
The streets they are so nigh, love, the people walk about.
     Em       D      Em   D        Em       D       Em
They may look in and spy, love, so blow the candles out.

And if we prove successful, name it after me.

Treat it neat and kiss it sweet and daff it on your knee.
     G        D         G D      Em      D       Bm
When my three years are over, my time it will be out,
     Em     D           Em    D       Em      D       Em
Then I will double my indebtedness by blowing candles out.
